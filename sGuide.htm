Guide

(Information and images from GCFLearnFree.org)

Sign Up Download and install Skype Sign In Interface Manage Friend List Call or Video Call Sign Out

Sign Up


Go to the official website(www.skype.com) and click the "Get Skype" Button

Click "Join Us"

You can either sign up with your exiting Microsoft Account or create a new account by fill the information below

Click "I Agree - Continue" after filled information and verify your account in your email inbox, your account have been created

Back To Top

Download and install Skype


Go to the official website(www.skype.com) and click the "Get Skype" Button

Click "Get Skype for Windows desktop" if you are using Windows

Or if you are using Mac or Linux, just select your platform at bottom

Choose the location to store installer and click "Save"

Locate and select the installation file on your computer, then follow the instructions to install Skype.

Back To Top

Sign In


Enter your username and password in the login area(red box area)

The Skype window will appear.

Back To Top

Interface


You can edit your account by click the username on top left side

You can find your friend on your friend list

Your will see chat box after click your friend's icon on friend list

Back To Top

Manage Friend List


You can add your friend by search skype directory or via phone number

If you select "Search Skype Directory", you can input your friend's username and click "Search Skype"

If you select "Save a phone number", just input your friend's phone number into the column

Click the "Recent" button if someone is added you(image from Skype)

Click the pending user icon and confirmation box will pop up(image from Skype)

Simply click on the "Accept" or "Decline" buttons to add a new friend or reject the friendship invitation.(image from Skype)

Right click on the icon of user you want to delete and select "Remove from content"

Click "Remove" button to remove friend

Back To Top

Call or Video Call


Click the friend's icon you want to call

Select Video call or phone call to call

Back To Top

Sign Out


Click Skype > Sign Out

You will return back to the login menu
