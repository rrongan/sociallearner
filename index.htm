Learn 3 of The Most Popular Social Media On Our Website!

Decided to communicate with your family far away from you but do not want to spend money for telephone charge? Want to share your status, favourite songs or videos to other person? Using a social media is your best choice. In social media platform you can share anything or contact anyone you want.

What is Social Media?

"Social Media Explained Visually" by SayItVisually from YouTube

From Oxford Dictionary, social media is websites and applications that enable users to create and share content or to participate in social networking. For more detail, you can refer the “Social Media Explained Visually” video.

Introduction to Social Learner

In this website, you'll learn about how to use 3 of the most popular social media platform - Facebook, WhatsApp and Skype. You'll also discover some feature of these social media such as sharing your status or photos, instant communication with other people and more in this website.
