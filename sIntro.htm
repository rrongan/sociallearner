Calling

Skype to Skype calls

Call anyone else on Skype for free, anywhere in the world.

Group calls

Get a group of people together on one call – you can add up to 25 people.

Skype Number

Your friends call a number and you pick up on Skype wherever you are in the world.

Not on Skype or can’t pick up? Simply get your Skype calls forwarded to any phone.
