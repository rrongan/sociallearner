Guide

(Information and images from Free Facebook Tutorial and FacebookBrand)

Sign Up Sign In Interface Add, Accept and Remove Friend Sign Out

Sign Up


Go to the Signup Page on facebook.com (The signup page is on the main page of Facebook)

Fill in your information in the sign up column(Red box area)

Click "Create an account" button when you filled in your information

Fill in the security check and click the sign up button

Find your friend in your email address list(Additional step)

Fill in your detail information(Additional step)

Upload your profile picture(Additional step)

Go to your email mailbox and find the Facebook verification email

Click the verification link to verify your account

Now your account are created

Back To Top

Sign In


Enter your email address and password in the login area(red box area)

Tick the "Keep me logged in" selection if you are using your personal computer

Now you are logged in to your Facebook

Back To Top

Interface


You can use search box to find people, pages, place or group.

Friend requests, chats and notifications will pop up in this area if it is unseen.

The status of pages, friends, people or group you followed will be shown in Timeline

Your friends' recent status will be shown in status area

You can find your friend in the friend area and text box will pop up click on the name

You can upload your status or photos in this area

Back To Top

Add, Accept and Remove Friend


Input your friend's name into search box(red box area)

Click "Add as friend" when you found your friend's account

Click "Send Request" to send request for your friend

The profile of Facebook members you sent a friend invite to will show a "Awaiting friend confirmation" message instead of the usual "Add as Friend" button.

Click on the "Friend Requests" button when you receive a friend request

Simply click on the "Confirm" or "Ignore" buttons to add a new friend or reject the friendship invitation. Clicking on either button will remove the friend request.

Search your friend's account that you decided to delete

Click "Remove from friend" for both account list and confirmation box

Back To Top

Sign Out


Go to the menu on the navigation bar

Click "log out" to sign out your account

You will return back to the login menu when u signed out
